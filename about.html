Wanna know how our roots grew?
Hmmm...Where to start?
Deep Roots was started by Sheldon Crabtree after returning from Europe
and having seen the desire for African Objects d’Art.
The frustration of having no platform to promote his own art and craft
together with his unending enthusiasm and drive to promote South African products
and to help the regular guy, led to the creation of Deep Roots a proudly South African online gallery.
Been living under a rock?

Curious about what we're up to now?

Well. . .
Deep Roots is a proudly South African online gallery
promoting all aspects of local artistic talent.
Deep Roots welcomes all artists, entrepreneurs and free thinkers
to join our community so we can assist you on your way in this crazy world of ours. We promote and sell South Africa products, made by South Africans, locally and internationally.
Local talent is best!
We’re here to help the little guy earn a living or even a little change on the side.

Wanna be a part of this?
Anyone can advertise with Deep Roots free of charge.
When your piece is sold Deep Roots takes a small percentage and arranges delivery.
Win-Win
Bring your unique flair and style to Deep Roots.
Photos, paintings, drawings, wood work, jewellery, crafts, clothing – whatever.
Let Deep Roots be your platform to reach the world.
